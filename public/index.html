What is this?

Twitter is full of feelings. Machines might be able to help journalists find unique stories, sources, and context in those feelings.

Search for keywords (“taxes”, hashtags (“#njpolitics”), people (“@icijorg”), and places (TK it’s kind of annoying) and get back a sentiment analysis of matching tweets.
